Biography

Matthew Dean Burdette, better known as BLXST, is a Los Angeles based rapper, songwriter, and producer.

Early Life and Career

Mathew Burdette grew up in South Central Los Angeles, where he listened to West Coast/California Based Hip Hop. In 2019, BLXST released his single: Hurt, which went on to gain millions of streams. Later that year, BLXST, along with fellow Los Angeles-based rapper, Bino Rideaux, released a collaborative EP titled Sixtape.
